Together We Celebrate

Join us for our second annual Rally!
When: October 3rd Where: Buffer Zone

About the Festival

Together We Celebrate is a community festival dedicated to bringing people together through culture, music, food, and fun. Everyone is welcome to enjoy a day filled with excitement and connection.

  • Celebrates cultural diversity and unity
  • Open to families, friends, and visitors
  • Free admission for all attendees

Live Performances

Experience an exciting lineup of live performances throughout the day, featuring talented musicians, energetic dance groups, and cultural artists. From traditional performances to modern entertainment, the stage will be filled with music, creativity, and unforgettable moments for audiences of all ages.

  • Traditional dance performances
  • Live music from local artists and bands
  • Special guest performances throughout the day

Food and Drinks

Enjoy a variety of delicious meals, snacks, and refreshing beverages from local vendors and community food stalls.

  • Traditional Gambian dishes
  • Fresh fruit juices and soft drinks
  • International food selections

Festival Activities

Take part in engaging activities designed for people of all ages and interests.

  • Games and competitions with prizes
  • Arts and crafts workshops
  • Face painting and children's activities
